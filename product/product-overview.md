# AGRD (Autonomous Growth & Revenue Department)

## Description
A licensed platform that operates as an autonomous revenue department within growth-stage B2B companies. Built on proven patterns from production AI systems and Alex Hormozi's 100M Leads methodology, AGRD makes decisions, learns from every interaction, and self-improves like a senior growth team - with judgment, memory, escalation logic, and governed authority.

AGRD implements Hormozi's Core Four (Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, Content, Paid Ads) as parallel-executing autonomous systems that compound learning across all channels. Not automation duct-taped together, but corporate intelligence that compounds at scale - doing in days what would take human teams months, while remembering every lesson learned forever.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Revenue operations don't scale
AGRD operates with full autonomy within governed boundaries, making thousands of decisions daily using schema-driven generation and parallel processing. Humans become governors, not operators. Decisions execute in parallel across multiple channels simultaneously.

### Problem 2: Institutional memory evaporates weekly
Persistent memory system with state machine tracking remembers every objection, winning message, channel performance, and failed experiment across all time. Multi-model orchestration ensures context carries forward - knowledge compounds instead of resets.

### Problem 3: Growth teams are reactive departments, not autonomous systems
Strategy Brain uses thinking budget allocation (2,048-32,768 tokens by complexity) to continuously monitor markets, evolve offers, reallocate budget, and optimize like a VP of Growth + CFO hybrid. Not a campaign executor - a strategic decision maker.

### Problem 4: Humans waste cycles on low-stakes decisions
Clear escalation framework handles pricing, messaging, cadence, and booking autonomously using completion detection and multi-input handlers. Humans govern strategy and close high-stakes deals with full context, not manage workflows.

### Problem 5: Revenue resets with each campaign instead of compounding
Lifecycle Engine with asset versioning and batch generation treats every contact as a long-term asset, continuously nurturing, reactivating, upselling, and generating referrals. Revenue becomes self-reinforcing through institutional learning. Referral Growth Equation (% Referred Monthly - % Churned Monthly = % Compounding Growth) targets 25%+ referral rate for exponential compounding.

## Foundational Methodology: Hormozi's Core Four at Scale

AGRD transforms Hormozi's manual lead generation framework into autonomous parallel execution:

### The Core Four × AGRD Multiplier
**Traditional Approach**: One person, one method at a time, sequential testing, memory loss between campaigns
**AGRD Approach**: Multiple methods simultaneously, parallel optimization, institutional memory compounds forever

1. **Warm Outreach (1-to-1)** → Autonomous relationship mining + 3-way introduction generation
2. **Cold Outreach (1-to-1)** → Personalized sequences at 1,000+/day with 4-step proven patterns
3. **Content (1-to-many)** → 6 hook formulas, multi-platform posting, Give-Give-Give-Ask sequencing
4. **Paid Ads (1-to-many)** → Client-financed acquisition, What-Who-When framework, auto-optimization to 5:1+ LTGP:CAC

### Four Lead Getters (People Who Get You Customers)
Beyond doing advertising yourself, AGRD automates leveraging others:

1. **Customer Referrals** — 7 automated referral triggers (at purchase, milestone, positive feedback) targeting 25%+ monthly referral rate
2. **Employees** — Training systems (Document-Demonstrate-Duplicate), performance tracking, ROI calculation per employee
3. **Agencies** — 6-month learning extraction → team training → consulting transition framework
4. **Affiliates** — 6-step army building (Find → Offer → Qualify → Pay → Launch → Integrate) with whisper-tease-shout launches

### More-Better-New: Constraint-Based Scaling
AGRD continuously applies Hormozi's scaling methodology:
- **MORE**: Detect constraint, solve it, do 10x volume
- **BETTER**: Systematic testing to improve conversion at current volume
- **NEW**: Only when MORE and BETTER exhausted, expand to new channel/audience

Autonomous constraint detection identifies bottlenecks (audience exhaustion, ad fatigue, capacity limits) and triggers appropriate scaling lever.

## Key Features (5 Autonomous Cells)

### 1. Strategy Brain — Market Sensing & Decision Making
**Core Function**: Continuously monitor markets, extract pain points, evolve offers, reallocate budget
**Implementation**:
- **Pain Point Extraction**: Analyze customer feedback to extract 10+ pain points ranked by (Pain × Frequency) / Perceived Difficulty
- **Offer Evolution**: Apply Hormozi's 4-element framework (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort)
- **Budget Allocation**: Monitor LTGP:CAC across all channels, auto-reallocate to highest-performing methods (target: 5:1+ ratio)
- **Constraint Detection**: Identify bottlenecks using More-Better-New methodology, trigger appropriate scaling response
- **Model**: Gemini 3 Pro with thinking budgets (2,048 for decisions, 8,192 for offers, 32,768 for strategic analysis)

**Autonomous Decisions**: Which channel to scale, when to pause underperformers, which offers to test, budget reallocation thresholds

---

### 2. Offer & Product Cell — Lead Magnet & Value Stack Engineering
**Core Function**: Generate high-converting lead magnets and offers using proven frameworks
**Implementation**:
- **Lead Magnet Types**: Reveal Problem (assessments), Free Sample (trials), One Step (workshops) - selected by customer awareness level
- **Delivery Mechanisms**: Physical, Digital, Event, Human - optimized for scalability vs perceived value
- **Value Stacking**: Generate 5 bonuses + workbook + OTO using schema-driven generation, calculate total value vs investment
- **Guarantee Selection**: Unconditional (high trust), Conditional (balanced), Anti-Guarantee (premium) based on price point and positioning
- **Model**: Gemini 3 Pro for complex offer engineering, schema-driven output ensures parseable JSON

**Autonomous Decisions**: Which lead magnet type to create, optimal delivery format, guarantee strategy, pricing tiers

---

### 3. Distribution Engine — Four Parallel Executors
**Core Function**: Execute Core Four methods simultaneously with institutional learning
**Implementation**:

**Warm Outreach Executor**:
- Mine CRM for relationship scores, generate 3-way introductions
- 7 referral methods (one-sided, two-sided, at-purchase, negotiation chip, events, ongoing, unlockable bonuses)
- Target: 25%+ monthly referral rate for compounding growth

**Cold Outreach Executor**:
- 4-step proven sequence (Compliment+Value → Curiosity → Proof+CTA → Breakup)
- Volume: 1,000+/day with AI-generated personalization per prospect
- Completion detection token marks sequences done

**Content Executor**:
- 6 hook formulas (Big Promise, Secret Reveal, Mistake, Question, Story, Controversy)
- Give-Give-Give-Ask sequencing (3 value posts : 1 promotional)
- Multi-platform simultaneous posting (LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube)

**Paid Ads Executor**:
- What-Who-When ad framework for all creatives
- Client-financed acquisition (first purchase covers CAC)
- Auto-optimization to 5:1+ LTGP:CAC, pause if falls below 3:1
- Multi-format generation: HTML emails, SMS, landing pages, social ads
- Batch processing with uniqueness tracking (no duplicate creative)

**Models**: Gemini 2.5 Flash for prose, Imagen 4 for visuals, Gemini 3 Pro for strategic copy

**Autonomous Decisions**: Channel budget allocation, creative testing, audience expansion, format optimization

---

### 4. Conversation & Sales Engine — Multi-Input Qualification
**Core Function**: Handle all inbound interactions, qualify leads, escalate high-value deals
**Implementation**:
- **Multi-Input Handler**: Accept text briefs, file uploads (screenshots, documents), URL inputs (website audits)
- **Conversational Extraction**: 5-question onboarding with completion detection token [COMPLETE]
- **Lead Qualification**: Score prospects on Problem (pain), Money (budget), Authority (decision-maker), Urgency (timeframe)
- **Auto-Routing**: Score 8+ → human sales immediately, 5-8 → automated nurture, <5 → long-term sequence
- **Model**: Gemini 3 Pro for multi-modal analysis, supports inlineData for image inputs

**Autonomous Decisions**: Lead qualification scores, routing logic, when to escalate with full conversation context

---

### 5. Lifecycle & Compounding Engine — Long-Term Asset Nurture
**Core Function**: Treat every contact as long-term asset with continuous nurturing
**Implementation**:
- **State Machine Tracking**: pending → generating → completed → error for all assets
- **5 Lifecycle Stages**: Lead (not purchased), New Customer (no results yet), Active (engaged), Dormant (30+ days inactive), Churned (cancelled)
- **Automated Sequences**: Stage-specific nurture with triggers (time-based, behavior-based, milestone-based)
- **Affiliate Launch System**: Whisper-Tease-Shout pattern (Whisper: curiosity, Tease: value elements, Shout: CTA with urgency)
- **Asset Versioning**: Track campaign performance over time, identify winning variants

**Autonomous Decisions**: Which stage to transition to, when to trigger reactivation, optimal upsell timing

---

### Institutional Memory — Persistent Learning System
**Function**: Remember every interaction, continuously improve across all cells
**Storage**:
- Every objection + successful/failed responses with conversion rates
- Every winning message + channel + audience + timeframe
- Channel performance history + decay rates
- Failed experiments + lessons learned (never repeat mistakes)
- Asset versions + performance tracking

**Compounding**: Each campaign makes next campaign smarter. Patterns learned in Channel A applied to Channel B. Objection handling improves continuously. No memory loss between campaigns.

---

### Governance Framework — Human-AI Collaboration
**Boundaries**: Humans govern strategy, AGRD executes tactics
**Escalation Rules**:
- Financial: Spend >$10K/day, LTGP:CAC <2:1, unexpected cost spikes
- Customer: Enterprise deals >$50K, complex customization, NPS <3
- Strategic: New channel proposals, major pivots, brand risk
- Technical: System failures, data inconsistencies, API outages

**Weekly Review Board**: Humans review trends, approve strategic changes, set constraints for next week

---

### Multi-Model Orchestration — Task-Optimized AI
**Model Selection Strategy**:
- **Gemini 3 Pro + thinking** → Complex reasoning (market research, offer engineering, strategic decisions)
- **Gemini 3 Pro (no thinking)** → Logical flow (structural outlines, ideation)
- **Gemini 2.5 Flash** → Fast prose (ad copy, email content, social posts)
- **Imagen 4.0** → Visual design (hero images, cover designs, branded assets)
- **Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite** → Quick rewrites (minor edits, small tasks)

Optimize cost, speed, and quality by matching cognitive load to appropriate model.
