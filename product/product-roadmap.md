# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Strategy Brain
AGRD's decision-making core that monitors markets, extracts pain points, evolves offers, and autonomously allocates budget across channels.

### 2. Offer & Product Cell
Generates high-converting lead magnets, value stacks, and core offers using Hormozi's Value Equation framework.

### 3. Distribution Engine
Executes the Core Four and Four Lead Getters as parallel autonomous systems running multi-channel campaigns simultaneously.

### 4. Conversation & Sales Engine
Handles inbound interactions with multi-input support, qualifies leads using PMAU scoring, and routes opportunities to humans or automated nurture.

### 5. Lifecycle & Compounding Engine
Treats every contact as a long-term asset with continuous nurturing, reactivation, upselling, and institutional memory that compounds across campaigns.
